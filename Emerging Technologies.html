Varietal Mixture (VarMix) Technology

Varietal Mixture (VarMix) is a mixture of seeds of selected varieties with closely similar agronomic traits but with genetic dissimilarities. Agronomic traits pertain to maturity, height, and grain size and shape, while genetic dissimilarities refer to the distinct genetic makeup of a rice variety. These varieties have diverse functions to lessen the effects of both biotic and abiotic stresses.

Key Points

  • VarMix is a stopgap strategy in unfavorable rice-growing environments to overcome biotic (e.g. pest and disease) and abiotic (e.g. water) stresses. This is made possible by sowing mixed seeds of two or more carefully selected varieties.
  • VarMix combinations have comparable, sometimes better eating qualities vis-à-vis single varieties.
  • Above (e.g. rainfall and water table-monitoring, and pest and disease evaluation) and below-ground (e.g. root analysis, and screening under adverse conditions) investigations show that VarMix combinations effectively address common rice cultivation issues.

Riding Type Paddy Seeder

Riding Type Paddy Seeder is a mechanized seeding technology as an alternative to manual broadcast seeding method in order to improve seed establishment, reduce drudgery, and improved timeliness of seeding operation. It seeds pre-germinated seeds in straight row with distinct row space and uniform seed density over an area. Significantly reduce seed requirement from the prevaling farmer's practice down to 20-40kg/ha seed rate.

General Specification

Prime mover 6.5 hp gasoline engine

MultiCrop Reduced Till Planter

The MCRTP was modified by DA-PhilRice to suit local field conditions like those areas with heavy clay. The MCRTP was designed to work on soils prepared using minimum or conventional tillage methods. It is thus equipped with a shoe-type furrow opener with a wider slit that facilitates the flow of water during irrigation, especially for corn.

Improved Nutri Rice Milk

This choco milk flavored ready-to-drink beverage is made with buffalo’s milk and nutritious germinated brown rice. The germinated brown rice used for its development is Calatrava, a pigmented rice variety. Pigmented rice varieties are known to be rich in phytonutrients like antioxidants such as anthocyanins and other phenolic compounds known to help in preventing the risk of having certain cancers. It also contains gamma-aminobutyric acid (GABA) from the germinated brown rice which is known to have energy-boosting, health promoting, and mental-enhancing properties.

Improved Rice wine

Described as clear, full-bodied wine with luxurious alcoholic flavor, moderate sweetness and lingering finish; the PhilRice Tapuy is a product of intensive research of our institution since 1995. It is made of just three main ingredients: rice, water, and bubod (starter culture). It is used traditionally by our forefathers for important occasions like rice harvesting and weddings. With 14% alcohol, it gives just the right level of inebriation, with no hangovers and allergies the morning after.
